✦ About Maya

Crafting Visual
Narratives that Sell

Maya is a boutique UGC and product photography studio obsessed with one thing: making your product impossible to scroll past. We combine studio precision with creator authenticity to produce content that converts.

With over 340 campaigns delivered across beauty, wellness, food, and tech, we've built a reputation for colourful, textured, editorial-quality visuals that brands keep coming back for.

Every shoot is a collaboration — we take the time to understand your brand, your audience, and your goals before a single frame is captured.
